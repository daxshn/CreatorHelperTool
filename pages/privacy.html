Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly how we handle your data — spoiler: we barely collect any.

Last updated: June 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

TubeTranscript does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our service.

We may collect limited, non-personal technical data including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URLs
  • Pages visited and time spent (via Google Analytics, if enabled)

2. YouTube URLs & Transcripts

When you submit a YouTube URL, it is used solely to fetch the transcript from YouTube's API. We do not store, log, or retain any YouTube URLs or transcript content that you submit. All processing is ephemeral and discarded after delivery to your browser.

3. Cookies

We may use cookies for:

  • Analytics (e.g., Google Analytics — anonymous, aggregated data only)
  • Advertising (e.g., Google AdSense — to serve relevant ads)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

4. Google AdSense

TubeTranscript uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to personalize ads based on your browsing history. For more information, visit Google's advertising policies.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • YouTube Data API – to retrieve captions from videos
  • Google AdSense – to display advertisements
  • Google Analytics – to understand site traffic (optional)

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect our website from unauthorized access. Since we do not store personal data, the risk to users is minimal.

7. Children's Privacy

TubeTranscript is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be indicated by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
