What is TubeTranscript?

TubeTranscript is a free online tool that extracts the text transcript from any YouTube video. Simply paste a YouTube URL, click the button, and receive the full transcript in seconds — with no account, no payment, and no hassle.

We also offer a full suite of AI-powered creator tools to help you repurpose video content: from title and description generators to Viral Hook writers, Instagram Reel scripts, and more.

How Does It Work?

TubeTranscript uses YouTube's official caption/subtitle API to fetch the existing auto-generated or manually created captions for a video. The text is then cleaned up and presented in a readable, copyable format. We do not use speech-to-text AI — we use real YouTube captions, which means results are fast and accurate.
