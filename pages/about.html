What is CreatorHelperTools?

CreatorHelperTools is a free creator productivity platform providing transcript generation, hook generators, title generators, hashtag tools, reel tools, AI creator tools, and content workflow automation. Simply paste a video link or keyword, select a tool, and generate content instantly.

We also offer a full suite of AI-powered creator tools to help you repurpose video content: from title and description generators to Viral Hook writers, Instagram Reel scripts, and more.

How Does It Work?

CreatorHelperTools uses official caption APIs and advanced generative AI models to fetch transcripts and write high-converting hooks, titles, hashtags, and script outlines. We combine real captions with state-of-the-art AI generation to give you perfect results.
